Measurment View Includes Seven Tabs

Introducing the Measuring View: A Key Feature of our Relay Testing Device at Vebko AmirKabir

Relay testing plays a vital role in ensuring the reliability and effectiveness of protective systems. To address the challenges associated with testing stages and conditions, we have developed a powerful feature called the "Measuring View" in our Relay Testing Device.

The Measuring View encompasses seven subsets, each dedicated to a specific task, aimed at providing comprehensive evaluation and confidence in the testing process. Let's explore these subsets in detail:

Evaluation of Specific Event Timing: Accurately assess the timing of critical events within the system, enabling precise analysis and troubleshooting.

Evaluation of Binary Input Status: Monitor and verify the status of values present on binary inputs, ensuring proper functioning and performance of the device.

Evaluation of Incremental or Decremental Ramps: Evaluate and measure the increase or decrease of current, voltage, or frequency, based on the protective function under examination, to ensure accurate and reliable operation.

Evaluation of Functional and Logical Performance: Assess the functional and logical performance of the device, taking into account the specific test conditions, to ensure its proper operation as per requirements.

Evaluation of Applied Transient Conditions: Evaluate the device's response and performance under transient conditions during protective testing, enabling effective testing of protection mechanisms.

Evaluation of Power Swing Functions: Thoroughly examine and evaluate the device's Power Swing functions, ensuring reliable operation during power system disturbances.

Evaluation of Parameter Values: Assess the values of specific parameters among the signals present at a particular time during testing, enabling precise measurements and analysis.

With these advanced features incorporated into our Relay Testing Device, you can confidently perform accurate assessments and efficient testing of protective systems, ensuring their reliable operation in critical situations.
